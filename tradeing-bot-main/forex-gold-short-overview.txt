Forex Gold (XAUUSD) - Short Bot Overview

- Strategy: Multi-factor score (Stoch RSI + trend + momentum candle + volume + support/resistance position).
- Trade trigger: Opens BUY/SELL only when score is strong enough (min score 3.5).
- Lot size: Fixed 0.01 lot per trade (no dynamic lot sizing).
- Entry pricing: BUY at Ask, SELL at Bid.
- Max open trades: 2 at a time.
- Initial risk setup: TP 45%, SL 60% from entry-price formula.
- Position protection:
  - Move SL to breakeven around +20% profit.
  - Start trailing around +30% profit.
  - Trail keeps about 10% below peak profit.
- Safety controls:
  - Crash detection can close all positions.
  - Cooldown pause after crash event.
  - Daily loss and daily trade limits are enforced.
- Scan speed: Checks market every few seconds in a loop.
